Mawsmai Cave Tour

04 Nights | 05 Days Shillong, Dawki, Cherrapunjee

Tour Overview

Journey through the vertical landscapes and subterranean wonders of Meghalaya. This 5-day package is built around the famous Mawsmai Limestone Caves, offering a perfect blend of high-altitude sightseeing in Cherrapunjee and the pristine, clear waters of Dawki. Travel in style and comfort with our professional hill drivers.

Tour Highlights

  • Mawsmai Limestone Cave Exploration
  • Dawki River Boating (Umngot River)
  • Scenic Umiam Lake (Barapani) Stop
  • Nohkalikai & Seven Sister Falls
  • Mawphlang Sacred Forest Trek
  • Elephant Falls & Shillong Peak
  • Don Bosco Museum & Cathedral
  • High Quality 3-Star Stays
