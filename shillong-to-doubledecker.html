KR Northeast Travel offers an online service for Shillong to Double Decker Living Root Bridge cab booking at an affordable price. Book your journey online in advance for the best rates and confirmed availability. We provide one-way drops to the Tyrna trek point, multicity packages, and full-day sightseeing roundtrips.

The road trip from Shillong to the base of the Double Decker bridge is one of the most scenic drives in the Khasi Hills. Hiring a chauffeur-driven cab is the most comfortable option, allowing you to relax before starting the famous 3,500-step trek down to Nongriat village.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the car options available for this trip?

For small groups, we offer 4-seater AC sedans (Dzire, Etios). For larger families or groups, we recommend our 6-7 seater SUVs (Innova, Ertiga). For groups larger than 7, 13-17 seater Tempo Travellers are available.

Can the cab stay overnight while we trek?

Yes, we provide multi-day packages where the driver can wait for you at the base point (Tyrna) while you stay overnight in Nongriat village. Please call our manager for specific overnight stay packages.

Our 24/7 support line is available for both pre-booking and urgent taxi requirements to Tyrna. Call us for a reliable hill driving experience.
