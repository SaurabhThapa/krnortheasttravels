Shillong - Dawki - Mawlynnong

04 Nights | 05 Days Shillong, Dawki River, Cleanest Village

Tour Overview

Experience the magical combination of crystal-clear waters and sustainable village life. This tour takes you from the beautiful capital of Shillong to the emerald Umngot River in Dawki and finally to the world-famous Mawlynnong village. Perfect for those who wish to see the natural marvels and clean living of the Khasi people.

Tour Highlights

  • Dawki: Boating in Umngot River
  • Mawlynnong: Asia's Cleanest Village
  • Single Living Root Bridge Visit
  • Indo-Bangladesh Border (Tamabil)
  • Umiam Lake (Barapani) Scenic Stop
  • Wards Lake & Lady Hydari Park
  • Don Bosco Museum & Cathedral
  • Local Handicraft Shopping
